About CD Projekt RED

Founded in 2002, CD PROJEKT RED is a game development studio based in Warsaw, Poland. We are the creators of The Witcher series of games and Cyberpunk 2077. Our mission is to tell captivating stories and create unforgettable RPG experiences for players worldwide.
